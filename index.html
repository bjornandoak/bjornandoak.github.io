About

Welcome to Bjorn & Oak, a one-man workshop rooted in craftsmanship, heritage, and heart. The name's Chris. I'm a husband, father, and craftsman reviving the tradition of honest, handmade furniture and goods. With strong Viking roots, a bear’s spirit, and a love of oak, every piece I make is built to last and made to matter.
